How it Works

Experience the magic of augmented reality in three simple steps! Follow the instructions below.


Download the App

Down the Artivive application to your phone or tablet. Dont worry, this will only take a few seconds! Once the application has downloaded, open on your device.


Find a Plaque

With Artivive open, point your phone or tablet at one of the many sponsorship plaques around the exhibits. These plaques are bright orange, blue, and purple. Pointing your device at these signs will trigger the augmented reality fun!


Point and Enjoy!

Enjoy even more fun and learning with the added exhibit features. Scan your surroundings with your phone to interact. How many suprises can you find?

Towne Square

CMOO’s town square is filled with things to do and places to explore. Be a part of a pit crew at the Speedway, put out the fire at the safety station, grind apples in the cider barn, and much more! CMOO town square offers engaging, interactive experiences for all ages!



The Cloud Climber

Oswego is known for its Lake Effect snow, so it’s only appropriate that our two story cloud climber teach visitors about our local weather while letting them climb and play! Visitors can watch “water vapor” scarves move through the cloud and catch them as they emerge as “rain drops,” or they can go inside of the cloud to get their bodies moving. Younger visitors who might not be ready to climb can take in all of the action from the observation deck! (copy from CMOO, Cite?)
